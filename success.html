Competition
Success

As in the global economy, a spark of competition drives DECA members to excel and improve their performance. DECA’s competitive events directly contribute to every student being college and career ready when they graduate from high school. The potential for travel, recognition and awards for learning classroom content through DECA’s competitive events is a tremendous motivator — not to mention the scholarships and cash awards recognizing DECA members for outstanding achievement.

The DECA Competitive Events Super Success System is designed to help chapters formulate a monthly competitive events preparation plan to take your members’ performance from good to great! Hopefully, DECA will see your selfies with a trophy on stage at your local, regional, association conferences and maybe even the International Career Development Conference!

dECA COMPETITIVE EVENTS SUPER SUCCESS SYSTEM

The cycle of the five actions below will help your chapter build an amazing competitive events super success system. Utilize this strategy with the downloadable module to put together a competition plan for your DECA chapter.

SHARE

Share DECA's competitive events and what qualities generate success in them. Use the "Find Your Competitive Event" module on DECA+ to survey members' interests and then explore specific competitive events on DECA+.

SPIRIT

Get your members excited and help them understand why competition — and winning — is awesome!

STRATEGY

Give your members the tools and strategies they need to succeed, from study materials to personalized feedback. DECA+ is your ultimate resource for helping pull together so many tools, strategies and resources all in one place!

SUCCESS

Recognize the success in your chapter, from completing a practice exam to advancing to your association conference to winning at ICDC!

SURVEY

Evaluate your members and leaders to determine what works and what can be improved.

DECA+ ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
‍A DECA+ subscription is required for each DECA chapter wishing to access the resources within the DECA+ site. The login is to be used only by the chapter and its members, and the login cannot be shared beyond the chapter. Under no circumstances may any of the content, including but not limited to exams, scenarios or sample written entries, in whole or in part, or any item within be published or posted on any website. Under no circumstances may any of the content, including but not limited to exams, scenarios or sample written entries, in whole or in part, or any item within be shared or distributed beyond the specific DECA chapter that has purchased the subscription.  Any chapter(s) which violates the DECA+ distribution and use policy will not be allowed to participate in the current school year’s chartered association conference or the International Career Development Conference. If any exams or scenarios are posted on a chapter or chartered association website in way outside of the distribution and use policy, the chartered association will not be eligible to purchase exams or receive scenarios from DECA Inc. for a period of one year for the first offense. Furthermore, further access to DECA+ by the chapter(s) in violation will be prohibited effective immediately for a period of no less than three years.
